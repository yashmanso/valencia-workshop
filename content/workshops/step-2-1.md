**externalities**

the idea that businesses often push costs onto society or environment without these costs showing up in their balance sheet

- students can define it and give textbook examples, but struggle to identify them in real products or businesses. the concept feels abstract


**task**

in pairs, which tool(s) from the toolbox would you use to make externalities tangible for these students? how do you motivate it? document your process
